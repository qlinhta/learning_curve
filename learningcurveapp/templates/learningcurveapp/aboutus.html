About Us

Our mission is to bring a centralized personal online learning space (MOOC) for learners

Our story

Welcome to our new online learning space! As students ourselves, we understand the need for a personalized and centralized platform to support your learning journey. That's why we've created an open source MOOC platform that's tailored to your individual needs.

At our master 1 MIAGE at Paris Dauphine University in 2022-2023, we noticed that while there are many online courses available, there is a lack of centralized and personalized resources for learners. That's where our project comes in. As a team of four dedicated students, we're proud to introduce the first platform of its kind - a centralized personal online learning space that's specifically designed to meet your needs.

Whether you're a student looking to enhance your knowledge, a professional seeking to upskill, or simply someone who loves to learn, our platform is here to help. With our intuitive interface, you can easily navigate through various courses, create personalized learning paths, and track your progress - all in one place.

We believe that learning should be accessible to everyone, which is why our platform is open source. We invite you to join our community and help us create a space that truly meets the needs of learners everywhere.
